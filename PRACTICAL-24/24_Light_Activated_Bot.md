AIM :

  Make Scoobot to move forward, when the light is in ON State.

COURSE OF ACTION :

Connect +5v & Gnd power supply from Arduino Uno to LDR Sensor Module.
Connect Output Pin from LDR Sensor to D3 pin in Arduino Uno
Connect D11, D10, D9 & D8 pins from Arduino UNO to Motor Driver In1, In2, In3 & In 4.
