AIM : 

  To make Scoobot Navigation by using TV Remote
  
COURSE OF ACTION :

Connect +5v & Gnd power supply from Arduino Uno to RC5 Decoder Module.
Connect D0, D1, D2 & D3 Pins from RC5 Decoder to D11,D10,D9 & D8 pins in Arduino Uno
Connect D7, D6, D5 & D4 pins from Arduino UNO to Motor Driver In1, In2, In3 & In 4 Pins.
