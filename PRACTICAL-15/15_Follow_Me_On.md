AIM : 

  To make scoobot move forward when there is obstacle in front of the robot, else it need to be in stop position.

COURSE OF ACTION : 

Connect +5v & Gnd power supply from Arduino Uno to IR Sensor Module.
Connect Ouptput Pin from IR Sensor to D3 pin in Arduino Uno
Connect D11,D10,D9 & D8 pins from Arduino UNO to Motor Driver In1, In2, In3 & In 4.
