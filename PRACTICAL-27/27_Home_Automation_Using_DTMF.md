AIM :

  To make electrical devices on / off by using DTMF Module
  
COURSE OF ACTION :

Connect +5v & Gnd power supply from Arduino Uno to DTMF Module.
Connect +Vcc & Gnd power supply from Arduino Uno to 4 Channel  Relay switch module.
Connect Q1, Q2, Q3 & Q4 Pins from DTMF Module to D11,D10,D9 & D8 pins in Arduino Uno
Connect D7,D6,D5 & D4 pins from Arduino UNO to 4 Channel  Relay switch module In1, In2, In3 & In 4.
