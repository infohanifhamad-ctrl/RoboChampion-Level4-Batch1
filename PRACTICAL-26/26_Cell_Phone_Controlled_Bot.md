AIM :

  To make Scoobot Navigation by using Cell Phone
  
COURSE OF ACTION : 

Connect +5v & Gnd power supply from Arduino Uno to DTMF Module.
Connect Q1, Q2, Q3 & Q4 Pins from DTMF Module to D11,D10,D9 & D8 pins in Arduino Uno
Connect D7, D6, D5 & D4 pins from Arduino UNO to Motor Driver In1, In2, In3 & In 4 Pins.
